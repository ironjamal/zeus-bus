Our Roles &
Respect.

The Student's Role

01

Respect the Space

Keep the bus clean. Use trash bins and avoid leaving personal items behind to ensure a comfortable ride for everyone.

02

Volume Control

Maintain a moderate noise level. Use headphones for music or videos to respect those who may be studying or resting.

03

Safety First

Remain seated while the bus is in motion. Avoid distracting the driver and follow all boarding instructions.

04

Punctuality

Be at your designated stop 5 minutes early. Respecting the schedule helps everyone get to class on time.

The Zeus Goal

By following these roles, we create an environment that supports every student's academic journey.
